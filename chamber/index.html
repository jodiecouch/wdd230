Albion on TV

See Albion on TV and discover a little history about what is now the Haunted Mansions of Albion and the Albion Campus Retreat located on what was once the Albion State Normal School campus. You can see the PBS special that aired June 1, 2021, Albion Normal: A Teachers College There is also a nice story by KIVI TV, Restoring Albion Normal School , that covers some history of the campus and the wonderful ongoing restoration.
