Personas and Scenarios

History and Genealogy Buffs

Web savvy person used to researching web sites to discover history. Likely using a desktop or a tablet since they do a lot of research on their device. Age is likely 35-75. Education usually consists of some form of secondary education or training past high school. History buffs could be any ethnicity. Genealogist interest typically caucasian as that is the majority of history available to this area.

Scenarios

  • My great grand-mother attended Albion State Normal School. I've seen the buildings. I'd like to know more about it.
  • I am intersted in the history of DL Evans bank.

Outdoor Enthusiast

May or may not be very internet savvy. Most likely viewing the site from a mobile device. Could be adolescent to 70 years old. Any level of education. Hunters, skiers, hikers, mountain bikers.

Scenarios

  • I'll be skiing at Ponerelle and I want to know the forecast and the road conditions in the area.
  • My family and I will be hiking in the nearby mountains and we want to know what trails are open, and what restaurants are available in town.
  • I will be hunting near by and I want to know what types of accomodations are available in the area.

Thrill Seekers

Internet savvy as the majority of this group will be ages 13-28. Most will be viewing on mobile device. Any ethnicity and a wide range of educational backgrounds.

Scenarios

  • My friends and I are planning to visit the Haunted Mansions of Albion and we want to know times of operation and where we can eat after our visit.
  • I am looking for haunted places in Idaho and heard about Albion Haunted Mansions. I want to know more.
