Personas:

History and Genealogy Buffs

Web savvy person used to researching web sites to discover history. Likely using a desktop or a tablet since they do a lot of research on their device. Age is likely 35-75. Education usually consists of some form of secondary education or training past high school. History buffs could be any ethnicity. Genealogist interest typically caucasian as that is the majority of history available to this area.

    Scenarios
  • Someone searching for history on Albion State Normal School, perhaps a relative attended.
  • Someone researching DL Evans bank.
  • Someone researching Idaho colleges of the past .
  • I am researching Idaho history.

Outdoor Enthusiast

May or may not be very internet savvy. Most likely viewing the site from a mobile device. Could be adolescent to 70 years old. Any level of education. Hunters, skiiers, hikers, mountain bikers.

    Scenarios
  • Skiiers and snowboarders wanting to know how much snow. Forecast and business hours of Ponerelle.
  • Hikers and mountain bikers wanting to know weather, what trails are open, and available amenities in town.
  • Hunters wanting to know weather and available ammenities.

Thrill Seekers

Internet savvy as the majority of this group will be ages 13-28. Most will be viewing on mobile device. Any ethnicity and a wide range of educational backgrounds.

    Scenarios
  • I want to know the times available to visit the Haunted Mansions of Albion.
  • I am looking for haunted places in Idaho and heard about Albion Haunted Mansions. I want to know more.
