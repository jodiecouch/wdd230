Our Roots

Mr. Espinota, a farmer by trade, came to California in the 1980s to manage a farm in Salinas Valley. He fell in love with the beautiful landscape and diverse cultures that make up California. In his free time he began exploring all that California has to offer. Eventually he settled in Carlsbad with his family.

Through his work in farm management and logistics Mr. Espinota has made many friends and aquaintances. It was not uncommon for these friends to share seasonal crops and specialty items from the areas they were traveling through. In time Mr. Espinota became known as a source for the freshest California produce without having to travel out of the area.

Mr. Espinota soon saw the demand for California fresh foods locally. He had also built a vast network of friends and partners in the agricultural industry throughout California. Combining these ideas he began selling fresh produce in a roadside fruit stand on Sunny Street in 1995. That roadside stand grew into what is now Bountiful Foods at our Carlsbad location.

With an increases in population and food awareness the demand for fresh, local and organic produce keeps growing. Recognizing the need for more locations throughout the South Coast region Bountiful Foods returned to it's roots, the roadside fruit stand. Today we provide that roadside fruit stand via five food trucks that serve the area. The food trucks allow us to offer our very popular juice bar where ever we may go.

We look forward to serving you. Bountiful Foods continues to provide innovative ways of serving our community and customers. Yesterday, today and tomorrow we remain true to our roots with fresh, local, organic produce.
