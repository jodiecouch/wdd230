Bountiful Foods

California provides one of the most abundant and diverse agricultural environments in the world. California is one of the few places where five major climate types occur in close proximity. Here, the Desert, Cool Interior, Highland, and Steppe climates border a smaller region of Mediterranean climate.1 California’s agricultural abundance includes more than 400 commodities. Over a third of the country’s vegetables and three-quarters of the country’s fruits and nuts are grown in California. 2

The dairies in Tulare county, berry and produce fields of Salinas, vineyards in Napa Valley, almonds from San Joaquin, orchards in Fresno County, beef of Shasta Cascade region of Northern California, local seafood all along the coast, all combine to make California a bountiful state. Bountiful Foods brings all of it together for your convenience. You can find us at our Carlsbad store and farmers market. Know what you want: order online and pick it up at in Carlsbad or one of our many food truck locations serving the South Coast region.

Looking for a delicious, refreshing drink? We offer a juice bar at all of our locations.

Bountiful Foods provides fresh, organic and sustainable produce locally grown and raised in the state of California. For your conveniece, we also work with national organic wholesalers to provide fresh produce that doesn't grow in California, such as bananas, mangoes and more.

Looking for a unique gift? Bountiful Foods offers unique items create by California artists. We also offer a unique assortment of hand crafted jams, jellies and other edible treats made from California produce and created by Californian food crafters.
